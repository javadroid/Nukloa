Proof of stake (POS)

Holders of $NUD can validate blocks of transactions based on the tokens a validator stakes. Depositing you $NUD in the protocol safety module is the act of staking. Staking serves as a tool for risks reduction in the event of a shortfall. Stakeholders in the safety module will receive safety incentives as a compensation for this.
The initial stakers rewards are 5000 NUD per day. Before the end of the three-month (90-day) schedule, votes will be cast on the quarterly allocation date for the safety incentives. The current allocation will continue until a vote or NUD reserve is depleted.

FAQs

What is Nukleon?

$NUD, also known as Nukleon Decentralized Orbital, is a cryptocurrency project that aims to create a decentralized AI exchange platform for trading digital assets. The platform is built on the Ethereum blockchain and uses smart contracts to execute trades automatically without the need for intermediaries, while maintaining their privacy and security. $NUD token is the native cryptocurrency of the platform and is used to pay for transaction fees and other services. It can also be used to stake on the platform and earn rewards. The project has a strong focus on community involvement and aims to provide users with a user-friendly interface and easy-to-use AI tools for trading. Overall, $NUD aims to provide a decentralized alternative to traditional centralized exchanges, giving users more control over their assets and trading activities.

Who owns Nukleon?

The ownership of NUKLEON has been released, and the community has taken collective ownership. There are no controlling entities like a CEO, VC, or board members. Additionally, funding or contributions are non-compulsory, and the final say in determining the future course of the project rests with the community itself.
